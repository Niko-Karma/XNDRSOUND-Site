### 1. CURRENT STATE ANALYSIS
*   **Theme:** "Urban Collage" (Dark Mode, Neon Red Accents, Monospace Typography).
*   **Structure:** Single-page scroll (`#hero`, `#bio`, `#music`, `#gallery` placeholder, `#contact`).
*   **Tech Stack:** Flask (Backend), HTML5/CSS3 (Frontend), FontAwesome.
*   **Brand Identity:** Moody, Dark Ambient, Street/Urban.

### 2. DESIGN SYSTEM BASELINE
*   **Typography:** 'Anton' (Headers) & 'Roboto Mono' (Body).
*   **Palette:**
    *   `#0a0a0a` (Background)
    *   `#ff3333` (Neon Accent)
    *   `#ededed` (Text)
    *   `#c2a878` (Warm Accent - usage currently low)

### 3. OPPORTUNITIES FOR ENHANCEMENT

#### A. Visual Dynamics (The "Wow" Factor)
*   **Micro-Interactions:** The site is currently static. Consider adding:
    *   Hover effects on gallery items (zoom/grayscale-to-color).
    *   Scroll-triggered animations (fade-ins for text).
    *   Glitch effects on headers to match the "XNDRSOUND" urban vibe.
*   **Hero Section:** Currently static images. Could benefit from a video background or parallax effect.

#### B. User Flow & Experience
*   **Music Player:** Currently relying on Spotify embeds. A custom, persistent audio player at the bottom of the screen could allow music to play while browsing.
*   **Gallery Expansion:** The `#gallery` is a placeholder grid. Design a lightbox experience or a masonry layout for better photo viewing.
*   **Mailing List:** Currently a separate link. Designs for an embedded form (modal or inline) would reduce friction.

#### C. Mobile Experience
*   **Navigation:** The overlay menu is functional but basic. A slide-out or bottom-sheet menu might feel more "app-like" and modern.
