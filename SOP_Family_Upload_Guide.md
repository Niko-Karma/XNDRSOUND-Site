# Family Member Upload Guide: Adding Images to the Site

**Goal:** Upload your new photos so they can be added to the website.

---

### Step 1: Login to Cloudinary
1.  Go to: **[Cloudinary Login URL]** (I will provide the credentials separately).
2.  Login with the shared email and password.

### Step 2: Upload Your Images
1.  Click on the **"Media Library"** tab on the left sidebar.
2.  (Optional) Create a new folder for the photoshoot (e.g., "Brooklyn_Shoot_2025") to keep things organized.
3.  Click the teal **"Upload"** button in the top right.
4.  Drag and drop your photos into the window.
5.  Wait for them to finish uploading.

### Step 3: Get the Links
1.  Once uploaded, hover over an image you want to use.
2.  Click the **link icon** (looks like a chain) to copy the URL.
3.  Paste this link into an email, text, or spreadsheet for me.
4.  **Repeat** for all the best shots you want on the site.

### Step 4: Tell Me About the Photos
For each batch of photos, let me know:
*   **Title/Theme**: (e.g., "Neon Nights", "Studio Vibes")
*   **Key Tags**: (e.g., urban, black and white, featured)
