THE SOUND WE CREATED

With melodic flows and a vulnerable pen, XNDRSOUND (also known as Xander) is an artist from Randolph, MA who brings his stories of love and life through the music. Far from lonely but still searching for something, Xander takes each song as an opportunity to open up. Blending elements of R&B and a dark ambient hip-hop sound, Xander draws on the emotion of moody open beats to set the foundation to tell his tales.
